About

Austin Lawrence is a recent graduate with a Master of Science in Robotics student from Northwestern University. His ultimate career goal is to solve a problem affecting over 10^9 people, particularly in fields of sustainability and urban congestion. His work has been featured through the New York Times and NPR via his first ag-tech startup, Future Tech Farm. Additional to his work in food production, Austin has involved in myoelectric prosthesis research at RIC, durable medical manufacturing, as well as automotive manufacturing. Currently a Robotics Research Engineer at Disney Research Pittsburgh, Austin is also a prospective PhD student in fields of machine learning, dynamics, mechatronics, and manipulation.
